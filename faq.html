Real answers to real questions from real musicians.

What is Roadie?

+

Roadie is your own AI artist manager. Not a chatbot — an autonomous operator that lives on Telegram and handles the business side of your music career 24/7. It books shows, manages your social media, tracks your finances, pitches playlists, plans releases, and checks in with you proactively. It's like having a full-time manager for a fraction of the cost.

How does it actually work?

+

You talk to your Roadie through Telegram — just like texting a friend. Tell it about your goals, upcoming shows, music links, and it takes it from there. It doesn't wait for you to ask — it proactively checks in, reminds you about deadlines, drafts venue outreach emails, plans your social content, and brings you opportunities. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.

What's the difference between the tiers?

+

Starter ($200 + $29/mo): Core AI manager — show booking, social media, merch tracking, financial overview, fan engagement. Perfect for artists who mainly play live.

Pro ($500 + $49/mo): Everything in Starter plus streaming analytics, playlist pitching, Meta/YouTube ads, release planning, email marketing, and press kit generation. For artists serious about growing online.

Label ($1,000 + $99/mo): The full suite. Everything in Pro plus contract templates, sync licensing help, multi-artist management, festival submissions, and a self-evolving AI brain that dreams, reflects, and brings you opportunities proactively.

+

We offer a local install option for $350 one-time. We set up Roadie directly on your Mac or PC. No monthly fees. You own it completely. Your data stays on your machine. Perfect for the privacy-conscious or tech-savvy artist who wants full control.

Is my data private?

+

100%. Your Roadie is completely isolated — it runs in its own environment with its own memory. No other artist's AI can see your data, and we never share or sell your information. Your conversations, your music, your strategy — it's all yours.

What AI model does it use?

+

If you want even better creative writing and deeper reasoning, you can upgrade to our Premium Brain (Sonnet) or Ultimate Brain (Opus) add-ons. Can it actually book shows?

+

Yes. Roadie researches venues in your target markets, drafts professional outreach emails, follows up automatically, and tracks responses. It learns which venues are a good fit for your genre and audience size. You review and approve before anything gets sent — it's your manager, not a rogue agent.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

+

ChatGPT forgets you every conversation. Roadie remembers everything — your shows, your goals, your style, your contacts, your entire career history. It has its own personality that evolves over time. It checks in proactively without you asking. It's not a tool you use — it's a manager that works for you 24/7. That's the difference between a search engine and a teammate.

Does it work with Spotify, Apple Music, etc?

+

Roadie can analyze your streaming data, help you develop playlist pitching strategies, plan release rollouts optimized for algorithmic playlists, and track your growth across platforms. It helps you make data-driven decisions about your music career.

What if I cancel?

+

No contracts. Cancel anytime. If you're on a hosted plan, your Roadie stays active until the end of your billing period. You keep all your data and conversation history. We'll even help you export everything if you want. No hard feelings — we want you to stay because it works, not because you're locked in.
