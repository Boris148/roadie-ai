Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about Roadie and how it works.

What is Roadie? +

Roadie is a fully autonomous AI artist manager built for independent musicians. It lives on Telegram, manages your career 24/7, and handles everything from show booking and social media to streaming analytics and financial tracking. Think of it as having a real manager — except it never sleeps, never takes a commission, and costs a fraction of the real thing.

How does it work? +

Your Roadie lives on Telegram. You message it just like you'd text a real manager. It's proactive — it checks in daily with updates, ideas, reminders, and opportunities it found for you. It remembers everything about your career and gets smarter every day as it learns what works for you.

After you sign up, we build your Roadie in 24-48 hours and it messages you first on Telegram. From there, just start talking to it.

What's the difference between tiers? +

Starter ($200 + $29/mo) — Core AI manager with show booking, social media, merch tracking, financial management, memory, and personality. Everything you need to stop doing it all yourself.

Pro ($500 + $49/mo) — Everything in Starter plus streaming analytics, playlist pitching, ad campaigns, release planning, email marketing, and press kit generation. For artists ready to grow seriously.

Label ($1,000 + $99/mo) — Everything in Pro plus custom landing pages, full ad management, contract templates, sync licensing help, multi-artist support, and festival submissions. Built for labels and serious operations.

Can I try before I buy? +

Each Roadie is custom-built for your specific career, so we don't offer a generic free trial. However, there are no contracts — you can cancel your monthly hosting anytime and keep the setup work we've done. If it's not for you, you're never locked in.

What if I want to self-host? +

We offer a local install option for $350 one-time. We install Roadie directly on your Mac or PC. No monthly fees. You own it completely. Your data stays on your machine. Perfect for the DIY artist who wants full control and doesn't mind running it locally.

Is my data private? +

Completely. Your Roadie runs in its own isolated environment — its own server instance, its own memory, its own workspace. Your data, conversations, and strategy never touch another artist's AI. We can't see your conversations either. It's your manager, not ours.

What AI model does it use? +

By default, Roadie runs on Gemini 2.5 Pro — a powerful, fast AI model that handles management tasks extremely well. If you want an even smarter brain, you can upgrade to the Premium Brain (Sonnet, +$15/mo) for better creative writing, or the Ultimate Brain (Opus, +$50/mo) for the most advanced AI reasoning and personality available.

Can it actually book shows? +

Yes. Roadie researches venues in your area and target markets, drafts professional outreach emails customized to each venue, sends follow-ups on a schedule, and tracks every conversation. You approve before anything goes out, but it does all the legwork that would normally take hours of your time.

How is this different from ChatGPT? +

ChatGPT forgets you after every conversation. It has no memory, no personality, no proactive behavior, and no understanding of your career. It's a general-purpose chatbot.

Roadie is YOUR dedicated manager. It has persistent memory of your entire career, develops its own personality over time, checks in with you every day without being asked, and is specifically built for music career management. It doesn't just answer questions — it operates autonomously on your behalf.

What about multiple AI models? +

Your Roadie defaults to Gemini 2.5 Pro, which is excellent for management tasks and comes included at no extra API cost. You can upgrade the brain at any time: Premium Brain (Sonnet) adds better creative writing for +$15/mo, and Ultimate Brain (Opus) adds the most advanced reasoning and personality for +$50/mo. Switch anytime.

Does it work with Spotify, Apple Music, etc? +

On the Pro and Label tiers, Roadie includes streaming analytics integration. It tracks your numbers across platforms, identifies growth patterns, spots which songs are gaining traction, and builds playlist pitching strategies to get your music in front of more listeners. It helps you understand your streaming data and act on it.

What if I cancel? +

No contracts. Cancel your monthly hosting anytime. You keep the setup work we've done and all the content your Roadie has created for you. If you're on the local install, there's nothing to cancel — you own it. We want you to stay because Roadie is valuable, not because you're locked in.
