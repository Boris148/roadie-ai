What is Roadie? +

An autonomous AI artist manager. Not a chatbot — an operator. Roadie actually sends your emails, posts your content, books your shows, tracks your finances, and checks in with you every morning. It's like having a full-time manager for $99/mo.

How is this different from ChatGPT? +

ChatGPT forgets you every session and can't do anything outside the chat. Roadie has persistent memory of your entire career, connects to your actual accounts (Gmail, Spotify, Instagram, TikTok, Calendar), proactively checks in daily without being asked, develops its own personality, and actually executes tasks — sends real emails, posts real content, researches real venues.

What accounts does it connect to? +

Gmail (to send and receive emails), Spotify (to pull your streaming data), Instagram (to post content), TikTok (to post content), and Google Calendar (to manage your shows and deadlines). Roadie walks you through connecting everything in about 5 minutes.

Will it post without my approval? +

Never. You approve everything before it goes out. Roadie drafts emails, creates posts, and prepares outreach — but nothing gets sent or published until you say go. It's your manager, not a rogue agent.

What genre does it work with? +

All genres. Hip hop, rock, country, EDM, R&B, pop, metal, indie, jazz — Roadie adapts to your specific situation, audience, and goals. It learns your voice and style and represents you authentically.

Can it really book shows? +

Yes. Roadie researches real venues in your target markets that book your genre, drafts professional outreach emails, sends them from your Gmail, follows up automatically, and manages your calendar. You review and approve everything before it goes out.

What happens in the first 10 minutes? +

Within 10 minutes of connecting your accounts, Roadie delivers: 5 real venues in your city that book your genre, outreach emails drafted and ready to send, your EPK/press page live and shareable, a full week's content calendar with captions, your Spotify data analyzed with actionable insights, and your financial tracker set up.

Is my data private? +

100% isolated. Your Roadie runs in its own environment with its own memory. No other artist can see your data, your conversations, your strategy, or anything else. Your data is yours alone.

Can I cancel? +

Anytime. No contracts. No cancellation fees. You keep all your data. We want you to stay because Roadie works, not because you're locked in.

What AI model does it use? +

GPT-4o by default — a powerful, fast AI model perfect for management tasks. Upgradeable to more advanced models if you want deeper reasoning and better creative writing.

No. If you can text, you can use Roadie. It lives on Telegram — you just talk to it like you'd talk to a real manager. Send it a message, get things done. Roadie walks you through connecting your accounts step by step.
