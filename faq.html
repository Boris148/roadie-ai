Everything You Need to Know

Real answers to real questions from real musicians.

What is Roadie? +

An autonomous AI artist manager that actually does the work — sends emails, posts content, books shows, tracks finances, and checks in every morning. Not a chatbot. Not a dashboard. An operator that lives on Telegram and handles the business side of your music career 24/7.

How is this different from ChatGPT? +

ChatGPT forgets you every session and can't do anything. Roadie has persistent memory, connects to your actual accounts (Gmail, Spotify, Instagram, TikTok, Calendar), proactively checks in daily, and actually executes tasks — not just suggests them. It's the difference between a search engine and a teammate.

What accounts does it connect to? +

Gmail (sends and manages emails on your behalf), Spotify (pulls your streaming data and analytics), Instagram and TikTok (creates and posts content), and Google Calendar (manages your schedule, show dates, and deadlines). You authorize each connection — Roadie walks you through it in 5 minutes.

Will it post without my approval? +

Never. You approve everything before it goes out. Roadie drafts, you confirm. It's your manager, not a rogue agent.

What genres does it work with? +

All of them. Hip hop, rock, pop, country, EDM, metal, R&B, jazz, classical, Latin — Roadie adapts to your specific genre, audience, and situation. It learns your voice, your market, and your goals. There's no genre limitation.

Can it really book shows? +

Yes. Roadie researches real venues in your area that actually book your genre, drafts professional outreach emails, sends them from your Gmail, follows up automatically, and manages your calendar. You review and approve everything before it goes out.

What happens in the first 10 minutes? +

5 real venues in your city researched, outreach emails drafted, your EPK/press page built, a week's content calendar created, your Spotify data analyzed, and your financial tracker set up. All within 10 minutes of connecting your accounts.

Is my data private? +

100% isolated. Your Roadie runs in its own dedicated environment with its own memory. No other artist's AI can see your data. Your conversations, your music, your strategy — it's all yours. We never share or sell your information.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. No contracts. Cancel anytime. Your Roadie stays active until the end of your billing period. You keep all your data. We want you to stay because Roadie works, not because you're locked in.

Can Roadie build websites and landing pages? +

Yes — included. Roadie has full coding ability built in. It can build websites, landing pages, electronic press kits, automate workflows, and create custom tools for your career. No extra charge. Think of it as having a developer on staff.

Can I send voice messages to my Roadie? +

Yes — included. Send and receive voice messages on Telegram with a branded AI voice. Talk to your manager like you'd talk to a real person — hands-free, on the go, while you're driving to a gig or in the studio. No extra charge.

What if I want to self-host? +

We offer a local install for $997 one-time. We set up Roadie directly on your Mac or PC. Same features, same everything — it just runs on your machine. No monthly fees after that. Your data stays 100% on your hardware. Perfect for labels or privacy-focused artists who want full control.
